OUR PROCESS

How We Turn Ideas Into AI

A transparent, collaborative methodology — from your first message to a live, optimised AI solution — built to deliver measurable results at every stage.

Step by Step

Our 5-Step Framework

Every project follows the same proven path — transparent, iterative, and built around your goals.

1
Discovery

Deep Discovery Call

We start with a free 30-minute call to understand your business, goals, current challenges, and where AI can create the most impact. No pitch — just listening. We ask the right questions so the solution we design actually solves the right problems.

Goal Mapping Pain Point Analysis Opportunity Identification
2
Strategy

Custom Proposal & Roadmap

Within 48 hours of the discovery call we deliver a detailed written proposal — covering solution architecture, AI model selection, timeline, milestones, and transparent pricing. You'll know exactly what you're getting and when before you commit a single dollar.

Solution Architecture Transparent Pricing Milestone Plan
3
Build

Iterative Development

We build in short sprint cycles with regular check-ins and live previews — so you're never left waiting to see progress. Feedback is incorporated fast. You're a collaborator in the build process, not a spectator waiting on a big reveal.

Sprint Cycles Live Previews Real-time Feedback
4
Launch

Deployment & Integration

We handle the full deployment — connecting your AI solution to your existing tools, CRMs, websites, or internal systems. We test thoroughly across devices, edge cases, and load scenarios, then hand over full access with clear documentation and a walkthrough.

Full Deployment System Integration QA Testing
5
Optimise

Continuous Optimisation

AI isn't a set-and-forget technology. After launch we monitor performance, track KPIs, and proactively surface improvements — model retraining, prompt tuning, new integrations, or feature additions — so your solution keeps getting smarter over time.

Performance Monitoring Model Retraining Ongoing Support
Why It Works

What Makes Our Process Different

Fast Turnaround

Most projects are live within 2–4 weeks. We work efficiently because we've built similar solutions before — and we don't waste your time on unnecessary meetings.

No Lock-In

You own everything — code, data, models. We build on open standards and hand over full ownership at delivery. No vendor lock-in, ever.

True Collaboration

You're involved at every sprint checkpoint. Our async updates and shared project boards mean you always know exactly what's happening and what's next.

Fixed-Price Clarity

No hourly billing surprises. Every project is scoped and quoted up front. If scope changes, we discuss and agree before any extra work begins.

Results-First Mindset

We measure success by your outcomes — leads generated, time saved, costs reduced — not by lines of code or features shipped. Metrics matter.

Model-Agnostic

We're not tied to any one AI provider. We pick the best model for your use case — GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, or open-source — and always match the tool to the task.
